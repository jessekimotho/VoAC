My buttocks vibrate. Wizkid shouts.I thrust my hand back there and yank my phone out. It's one of our great fans calling to ask about Friday.

"<em>Yo! Maze</em> what happened to Friday? "

"He got too loud at creation and was placed at the backrow of days of the week by God." I respond.

"No, I mean the usual thing you guys do. The.... The Child of Africa or something like that. " He persists. Obviously he didn't get my funny reply and neither does he the name of our blog.

"Yeeeeeaaah, about that. " I mumble having realised I hadn't prepared for this grilling. "There have been technical issues with the editing crew so we didn't make it. But we shall sure correct."

I lied. Through my two big front teeth.

When I zoned out on Friday while waiting for my guy who was supposed to do the Friday piece, I woke up later to realise he actually hadn't. So I slept and brushed it off ati maybe he had network issues and would post in the morning. Or maybe he had stepped on a knife while walking around in his bedsitter. You can never be sure of this bedsitters. You walk in tip toeing like it's a landmine just incase you step on a hot pan you had carelessly placed. Or a knife as I assumed with my homeboy.

I woke up on Friday. He hadn't posted. My inbox was full of disgruntled readers wanting to know why. I also didn't. So I decide to call my guy to know what's popping on his end. Maybe he had been invited at a barbeque and couldn't post.Heck, maybe he was the main spit at the barbeque and cant write while being grilled. He can't handle two commitments. But seriously, he has never had the ability to traverse various ends of the social spectrum.

You guys know TDH right? Tall, Dark and Handsome for those who went to Mukubu School for African Sons and Gentlemen. Now my guy is Tall and Dark. The H is missing. He is not that pleasant to the eye to be frank. He is 18 but he looks 40. But he has always has a fine damsel at his side. It pains me especially when this guy with with his look, a seething disposition of the face of a chameleon if you ask me, can get fine ghels easily. So at some point in my assumptions I think  he had decided to seek the wise counsel of a plastic surgeon. So I call him up.

Druuuuuu. Click.

"Brotherman!! " He shouts.

<em>Sidenote</em>.

( Let it be known I hate being called this by people visiting plastic surgeons).

"<em>Msee</em>, where are you? " I question. I hear a buzzle of activities in the background. Motorbikes mostly.

Motor- bikes huh? Kisumu. Bungoma. Githurai. Coast............ India. Aaaaah India. Yep. Yep. Plastic surgery!.

" <em>Usitense</em>, I am working on something and need to be away from humans <em>kiasi</em>" He says.

"Yeah, so much from someone who doesn't look like a human being himself."
No. I didn't tell him that though.

So we chat a bit as my FBI brain tries to decipher where he is. He refuses to let open his co-ordinates and says I am even very lucky to find him on phone.
I wish him good luck and all.

End of call.

"Damn!" I curse. <em>Sijamuuliza kuhusu post ya Friday.</em>

So I call him back. Number temporarily out of service. Yep! Looks like he was right. I was lucky to have found him on call. For someone whose face doesn't look right, he is right for once.

What would Magufuli do? Write a post for him?
So I go through the trailer he gave on Tuesday.
Politics.

Mehn, I don't know how to write about politics.....yet. What will I write? How some guy was slapped in 2007 at my hometown for mentioning Kibaki? He had been warned though. He was told that if as small as his fart smelled of Kibaki's name he would see. Or would I write about that guy whose onions were urinated on and farm full of sugarcane looted because he was a Raila fan and others weren't?

But no. My stories aren't what my guy might want to write. He is a serious person,accompanied with a serious face( pun totally intended). Maybe he wants to write why we should or should not vote. Maybe he wants to say how we only rant behind our keyboards about change and still retreat back to our tribal shells at the polling station.

I would never tell though what he wanted to write. So until he is found, untill he resurfaces from Githurai or India or wherever in the name of Pubungu pakwach he is, I can't guess. So his will be the post that never was.

PS. He will write about it though when he comes. You will even tell by the look on his face.
