You think that's the most hectic thing since Judas betrayed Jesus huh? Be humble and sit your calories down. Try to gain weight, or watch me try to gain weight since the days of Galileo. Boy oh boy! Have I tried. People say I am skinny but I'd like to say I am slim. I hate the ones who call me thin. They are the kind of people who say crap like " Me I am".

"Me I am asking you",  "Me I am thinking we should shave our armpits", "Me I was born in Nairobi not like you in Kisii", "Me I know good English" (rolls eyes).

These are the same people who say "<em>pahii"</em> instead of Paris. They think they were appointed by Zeus to direct our affairs. You try to say Paris and they are six feet up in your rectum with their "<em>pahii"</em> bullocks. Ooh please, you haven't even been to as far as Kakamega in your whole goddamn life. They are the ones who wore and tore down my self-esteem some time back.

I have beef with fat people (pun).I never realised it was a huge deal not having chunks of meat hanging around my bones. So I never bothered with how I looked till my teen years when fellas started mentioning how I should take eating seriously. I would come from school and my mother would hug me with a sad face asking why I was starving myself. Girls started falling for six packs and I only had a six pack of ribs. So getting a woman by my side became an uphill task. And heck, they can't be by my side lest they want to be poked by bones sticking out everywhere from my body. Yes, those are the kind of jokes non-slim people make. They poke my bubble of adulthood. You all choke me with your "please eat more" stories. Impunity and utter disdain is clogging my system.

But if it helps, I have tried to grab a snack here and there. I tried drinking raw egg thinking It would boost. But that stuff tastes like boiled sperms. With a light hearted disposition and a soul filled with optimism, I tried drinking boiled cooking fat and that crap couldn't go down my oesophagus. I really wanted something, something that could fatten me up with unfathomable immediacy. But no, nature just wanted me shamed all over for it and people create jokes. I almost went bananas. Okay, I did go eat bananas. I still am an avid eater.

And therefore friends would make jokes on it. Like last year,I wondered why my buddies were not crazily psyched up for my birthday like was the norm for theirs.You know, the way celebrations are provocatively exhausting.But not even a drop of water made contact with my skin. Seriously, they couldn't have been civilized overnight. Questioning one of them, he casually replied,

" Boss, you are purely made of bones, the cake was enough. If we poured water on you, this place will reek of soup"

OUCH.!....
<em>Yaani supu ya mifupa.</em>

And then there is this female friend of mine who dared me to remove my shirt. You see, I hate dares. You will dare me to remove my shirt and the next thing you know you are removing yours and , "<em>baaam</em>"! We have a junior. But I refused, citing that she would scream if she saw my six pack. And you know what she said? You want to know?(tears are welling up my eyes). That yeah, she would scream and run to me with food with possible pity that I was dying.

People make being slim feel like a disability. Like it should have road signs,

<strong><em>" Thin people passing, speed limit lower than their clattering of bones"</em></strong>.

I will walk into a bank and the queue is all the way to the back and people will gasp.

" You poor thing, Go ahead!"

"Almighty Zeus bless you fat people", I would reply.

And the guard will hold my hand and walk me to the front of the queue. Right there with pregnant women because we are now on the same level. As if I have been ineluctably caught in this savage circumstance of being skinny.

I admit I have had struggles. All these I have disguised with my cheerful demeanor. For instance, I cannot sit on a wooden bench for more than 10 minutes before my non-significant buttocks start to throb with pain. I am in constant conflict whether to wear a skinny jeans, which will outline my body structure like a pen with no ink, or a baggy one which will portray how clothes can't fit me. I cannot fight, one blow will dislocate my jaw bone. I have hidden myself indoors to preserve energy and see whether I will fatten. That's zero grazing right there and it never worked. Someone said if you touch my stomach you can feel my spinal chord. Ouch again.

You know what? To hell with getting fat and going to the gym. But hey, even if I am bones and you're fatty we have a similarity. We all get a boner. Ha. Or at least I can.

Happy Easter. Or whatever.
