The campus bachelor is an animal. A wild untamed animal whose ways of survival in the campus jungle cannot be contained even in several volumes of a NatGeo special. He hunts through the day and during the night; leaving you bemused and wondering whether he really ever has time to rest. The campus bachelor is unlike any other, he has no childish tendencies like a high-school teenager nor the disillusioned illusions of adulting that accompany the graduate bachelor.
<h5>Ah-ah. <span style="color: #ff9900;"><strong><em>No.</em></strong></span></h5>
He knows how to dodge the bullets that life shoots his way. Life gives him lemons, he eats the lemons and spits back the seeds in life’s bitchy face.

&nbsp;
<h5>For the campus bachelor is his own man.
<span style="color: #ff9900;">He answers to no one.</span></h5>
&nbsp;

Well, he answers to no one except his parents.
And his landlord maybe.
Apart from that, he answers to no one.
Okay, maybe a few his older siblings if he has one. And his caretaker sometimes. Otherwise, it is crystal clear that he answers to no one at all.

His phone is a normal Infinix or Tecno, large enough to keep him off the embarrassment of having a kabambe and cheap enough to feel embarrassed when he meets a ninja with an iPhone. He doesn’t really care about the phone type. Except when in a group of pretty damsels who are rocking high-end devices and tablets. Otherwise, he doesn’t really care. Well, maybe a bit if he needs to take a selfie with a lass he just met and the camera of his Infishit, sorry, Infinix, is 2MP. Then he has to care. But otherwise, he doesn’t, <em>as long as ninacall na ninachat sijali msee.</em> Yes, hajali msee. But he will always ask you how much you bought your phone for.
<blockquote>It’s not like he cares how much your S6 cost you, he just wants to estimate how many days of ugali mayai all that 50 Gs would have pushed him through.</blockquote>
<em>Mimi nikibuy simu ya more than 10K lazima ikuje na nudes. </em>Otherwise, as long as he accesses his WhatsApp he is fine with his smaller device. He used to have Whatsapp GB because he particularly liked the dark theme that came with it, but he had to delete it.

&nbsp;
<h4>His crush had put up a meme about guys who use GB WhatsApp wearing Samosa underwear. You know, those ones with the name Cowboy or Manchester United running across its top band.</h4>
&nbsp;

So, he laughed and laughed and deleted his GB. Otherwise, he doesn’t really care how his phone looks. Unless he has 15MB of data, then he will restrict the background access to use that data for a couple of hours for checking his fantasy football Whatsapp group. And sneak for some twitter banter about Nyakundi having his account suspended twice. When he takes a screenshot, he will rotate the phone and have it in landscape so that his screenshot looks like it’s from a tablet. Otherwise, he is fine. Ooh, he might also disable the background restriction, open his Spotify app so that his notification bar looks lit in the screenshot he sends you. Otherwise, he never cares about your opinion.

The campus bachelor doesn’t care about your opinion unless of course, it hurts him. If he writes a blog post he will say he doesn’t really care about how many people read it, but how much impact it had on the few who read it.

But he will constantly check the comments on facebook to see what you are saying. He will send the link to a few friends and he will not wait for their feedback. Although he will just ask what you thought about it, it’s not like he cares about your opinion. Someone else already told him he is the greatest writer since King Solomon wrote his mixtape in the bible (those songs were many!). Yeah, so he knows he is awesome. But he would really love to hear your opinion, and hers, and his, and yours and the other one’s. But it’s not like he cares about them. But he would love to hear them. If he has a joke he thinks is very funny, he will post it on twitter or maybe facebook or his WhatsApp status. If it takes 20 minutes before he gets retweets or a response, he will pull it down for reasons that are not him feeling bad that you did not find it funny.

The campus bachelor eats where he wants because he is not here to impress you. He will eat in Klabu and gobble down his ugali matumbo. Or in Blessed Hotel somewhere in the pockets of K.U. where he will down his ugali-omena and wash it down with tap water. He doesn’t care whether you are seeing him. Well, unless maybe you are his crush then he will eat chapo beef somewhere else with a fork and spoon. Though he really doesn’t mind who sees him eating mutura na supu right along the road. But he might not eat if he sees a pretty youngin he recognizes from Instagram. Not that she knows him or is afraid of what she thinks,
<blockquote>
<h4>But you don’t drink chang’aa in a den opposite Kenya Breweries.</h4>
</blockquote>
It’s not like that quote makes sense, so don’t overthink it.

The campus bachelor does not have a girlfriend because he thinks love is a bullet to the brain of those who hallucinate the idea that one human being was meant to satisfy the emotional requirements of another. But oh, how he wishes that bullet hits him sometimes. But he says he is his own man, not answerable of his ways to any girl. But of course, it would be awesome if someone’s daughter warmed his bed sometime. He has learned the art of patience when he does his hunting. After all, the dry spell he carries has him making calculated moves like a thirsty pregnant gecko in the Sahara Desert. He will be chatting up 20 mamis in his DM for 2 weeks so that is he fails, at least 2 of them will have fallen his way. He walks with a packet of sure condoms he picked up in the school hostel washrooms. What he has no clue is that they expired about a month gone. How would he know really?

Education is what brought the campus bachelor here. He studies sometimes, sometimes he relies on formation. His GPA is lower than his dressing skills. But he doesn’t care much. Or so he says. He believes innovation is everything, creativity is greater than education that is dictated to you by professors who are too lazy to explain what they are reading from the handout. He doesn’t have any innovative idea yet. But he will in good time. He talks about Zuckerberg, Bill Gates and Richard Branson being geniuses of their industries without college degrees although he has never come up with anything creative past copy pasting twitter jokes to his WhatsApp status. Exams might make him study and panic and run around looking for notes but that should not make you think he cares about education. Thoughts of dropping out and being rich have definitely crossed his mind, but then he will have to survive on his own. Not like he survives on other people, maybe his parent’s money a bit. But then he is an independent man. He tried to bet sometime back and he lost a lot. But he swears to Dedan Kimathi’s grave, <em>Siku moja hawa sportpesa nitawakuta. </em>He swears and curses like a ma’fucker. <em>Huyo Mhindi wallahi shit atanitambua.</em> We can only wait for his fantasies to come true.

For the night is dark and full of terror and the campus bachelor is broke and hopes for better.
